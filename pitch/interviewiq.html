How It Works

From zero to interview-ready in 4 steps.

01

Choose Your Arena

Pick from Coding, Behavioral, System Design, or General. Set your difficulty — Easy, Medium, or Hard.

02

Start the Session

The AI auto-detects your interview type from context and kicks off with a realistic opening question.

03

Practice Live

Converse naturally — type or use voice input. Responses stream in real time. Up to 30 messages per session.

04

Review & Improve

Save interviews, review history, export transcripts, and track analytics to see how you've grown.
