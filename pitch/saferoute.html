// how it works

Four steps.
Map click to safety score.

01

Open the Map

Leaflet.js renders a live interactive map of Mumbai or Delhi — red & orange incident pins overlaid on real OpenStreetMap tiles.

02

Click Any Area

User taps a location. Flask backend captures coordinates and queries the SQLite incident database for all nearby reports.

03

AI Scores Safety

Groq-hosted LLaMA 3.3-70B synthesizes incident density, time of day, and severity into a 0–100 safety score with natural language reasoning.

04

SOS Anytime

One-tap SOS always visible — surfaces 112, 1091 (Women Helpline), 100, and 1096 (Nirbhaya) instantly. No navigation needed.
