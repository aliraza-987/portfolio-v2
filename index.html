InterviewIQ
LIVE · TESTING PHASE
An AI-powered mock interview platform that puts you in a real interview before you walk into one. Coding, behavioural, system design, and general rounds — at easy, medium, or hard — with a model that doesn't let you off easy.
Built entirely solo. Flask backend with Groq's LLaMA 3.3-70B handling all AI responses — streamed live via Server-Sent Events so answers feel like a real conversation, not a page reload. SQLite tracks session history and analytics. Voice input lets you speak your answers. Dark and light modes because detail matters.

The API went through three iterations — Anthropic, then Gemini, then Groq — before the latency and cost profile made sense for a zero-budget deployment. Every architectural decision was made under real constraints.
FlaskGroq LLaMA 3.3-70BSQLiteSSE StreamingVoice InputPythonOct 2025
↗ VIEW LIVE GITHUB REPO
Why I built it
Interview prep is broken. Most platforms give you a list of questions and a text box — that teaches memorisation, not thinking on your feet. I wanted something that simulates the dynamic of a real technical interview: a model that follows up, pushes back, and doesn't accept a vague answer.

Building it forced me to solve hard engineering problems: streaming responses without a frontend framework, managing conversation state across sessions, keeping latency low enough that the UX doesn't feel like waiting. Those constraints made me a better engineer.

02
SafeRouteAI
LIVE · SOCIAL IMPACT
A community-powered women's safety map for Mumbai and Delhi. Real incident data, AI-generated safety scores, and one-tap SOS — built because the problem is real, the gap was obvious, and no one was filling it.
162+ verified incidents seeded from Mumbai Police reports, NCRB 2023 data, and NARI 2025 national survey data — all on a Leaflet.js interactive map with Stadia Maps dark tiles. Groq LLaMA 3.3-70B generates plain-English safety scores (0–100) per location in real time. Users can report new incidents across 5 categories with upvoting, and trigger SOS contacts (112, 1091, 100, 1096) in one tap.

OpenStreetMap Nominatim handles location autocomplete. Zero-cost infrastructure with a keep-alive strategy for near-100% uptime on a free Render tier.
FlaskGroq LLaMA 3.3-70BLeaflet.jsSQLiteOpenStreetMapStadia MapsPython
↗ VIEW LIVE GITHUB REPO
Why I built it
I grew up in Mumbai. I've seen what it looks like when good tools only reach people who already have everything. Safety information for women in Indian cities exists — buried in PDFs, police reports, disconnected surveys. Nobody had put it in one place, made it interactive, and made it free.

This project taught me that the hardest part of a social impact product isn't the code — it's the data. Finding, cleaning, and ethically using 162+ real incidents required as much rigour as the AI layer.
